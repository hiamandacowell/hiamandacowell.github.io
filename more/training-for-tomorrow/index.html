Training for Tomorrow: Concussion Assessment in Virtual Reality

Enriching Scholarship 2025, University of Michigan. Co-presented by Amanda Cowell, Melissa McCurry, and Eric Schreffler.

About the talk

"Inside the Mind: Exploring Concussions" is an immersive virtual reality experience designed for aspiring clinicians learning concussion assessment.

As product manager, I led user testing and iteration: gathering feedback and data on the experience's impact, and shaping the product based on what we learned.

The talk covers the design process, the learning goals built into the experience, and what we observed about its impact on hands-on medical training.
