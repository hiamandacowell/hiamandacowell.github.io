Enabling Student Success with Extended Reality

EDUCAUSE Annual Conference 2025, October 29, 2025. Co-presented by Amanda Cowell (University of Michigan), Samba Diop and Warren Goetzel (Georgia Institute of Technology).

About the talk

A presentation and panel session on how Georgia Tech and the University of Michigan use XR to support student success in higher education. I presented on CAI's design principles for XR experiences, using specific projects as examples of faculty and Center for Academic Innovation co-design in practice.
