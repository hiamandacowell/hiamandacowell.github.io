About

Cross-functional by design.

I'm a product manager with five years leading cross-functional teams into ambiguous territory across a startup and innovation centers. I love the puzzle of tradeoffs: a client brings a thing they want built, and every week becomes a series of decisions about scope, sequencing, and where user reality should shape the plan. Getting those calls right takes digging in until you understand both sides.

I come to product from mathematics, which turns out to be a real asset. I can hold a technical conversation with engineers and translate complexity for non-technical clients, sharpened by teaching calculus and statistics on the side. I'm also a PM who builds. I prototype my own tools with Claude Code because being hands-on keeps me honest about what's realistic to ask of a team.
